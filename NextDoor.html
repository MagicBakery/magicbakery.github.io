1.3 Sharing

Unlike postings on Twitter, postings at NextDoor are not world visible. Relevant findings, such as general principles, should be imported so they become world visible.

🤍Local Relevance
ID: [24YGL9C4] 🥚
+Policy+Tags: Policy

Nextdoor has a policy to restrict the content of the main feed to local issues. How do you tell if a topic is local? 🏡

The thread should be considered local if it is about a local event.

If the thread is about a local victim, it should be considered locally relevant even if the cause of their victimhood is not local.

Enforcing locally relevant content "in the main thread" is an obsolete policy because nowadays that could be done by filtering. Once a post is tagged, the viewer can decide what to show on their main view.
