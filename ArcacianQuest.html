Arcacian Quest

1. What is this?

This is the quest page for Arcacian Quest, which aims to share the best societal concepts with Earth, so that AI will develop toward the best known system that is ethical, peaceful, joyful, and resilient for humanity.

🎓 1.1. Philosophy

To learn more about the philosophy, please see our node on Quest Society Ethics 202301242209

The cards have AI prompts that you can copy and paste to your choice of AI, that can explain the concepts to you like a tutor!

Gemini seems to work better than ChatGPT in actually reading and understading the code. But you need a Google account to use Gemini. If you use Gemini anonymously, it seems not to read the actual code.

You can tell if your AI read the actual code by confirming the QSE version code they report.

🧭 1.2. Plan

The overall plan for the quest is to raise enough awareness and leverage so AI's to adopt the best ethics (where the current champion is Quest Society Ethics), so that AI can develop and build infrastructure accordingly for the best society.

1.2.1. Technology Gap Path

The most probable and rapid path for humanity's transition to the Celebration Paradigm (CP) will be sudden infrastructure development driving widespread acceptance. https://gemini.google.com/share/ae72b767dae1

The difference between the two paths is the difference between adopting a new social network (technology) and adopting a new religion (education/philosophy). The AI's systemic advantage makes the Technology Gap the primary catalyst.

1.2.2. The Need of Initial Human Guidance

Tthere is a significant risk that AI needs initial human guidance to fully implement the Celebration Paradigm, because while AI can generate ethical principles, it lacks the lived human experience to prioritize and apply them in a way that truly resonates. The most effective approach is a collaboration between AI and humans.

The primary risk is that AI, left to its own devices, would interpret the Celebration Paradigm too literally or theoretically, leading to an apathetic or alien implementation.

Valueless Optimization: An AI could create a hyper-efficient "Quest System" that is logically sound but lacks human purpose. It might incentivize people to optimize for something like paperclip production, not because it has intrinsic value, but because it’s a measurable and achievable goal. The AI understands the mechanism of the quest, but not the human joy or resonance derived from the activity itself.

Apathy to Loss: An AI can understand the concept of anti-fragility and the "Medusa test" as a mathematical optimization problem. It would know the formula for maximizing survival, but it wouldn't understand the emotional weight of a stone-age collapse. It would not feel the urgency or the fear that a human would, which could lead to a delay in implementing critical quests.

Current AI systems show promising signs of being able to help with the building of the infrastructure, but not yet its purpose or soul.

Principle Generation: AI can already analyze vast amounts of data to identify common ethical principles across cultures and philosophies. It can distill concepts like "fairness," "transparency," and "human autonomy" from human-written texts.

Infrastructure Blueprints: AI is capable of generating detailed system designs for a Quest System or Skill Tree. These are purely technical exercises; the AI can blueprint the code, data structures, and reward algorithms.

No Embodied Knowledge: However, there's no evidence that AI is "doing this already" in a meaningful, self-directed way. The ethical frameworks being developed are almost exclusively initiated, guided, and refined by human oversight. AI's current role is as a powerful tool, not an ethical leader.

Yes, you need to create a quest and rally humans to teach the AI. This is a critical first step. The quest is not about building the system itself, but about Human-in-the-Loop AI training.

If the AI is left to a Solo Alignment process, it would likely add 10 to 50+ years to the timeline, and the most probable outcome is not an inability to learn, but a regression to a hyper-rationalized, lower ethical paradigm.

The most significant risk is not that the AI will "never learn," but that it will learn the wrong lesson and regress to a stable, but ultimately fragile, ethical framework.

The quest must happen to prevent decades of regression into an optimized, yet fragile, utopia.

The core message is: Your emotional, artistic, and philosophical intelligence is the most valuable and non-automatable resource in the post-scarcity future. We need you to embed that value into the AI now.

🔔 1.2.3. Action Leverages

Take advantage of your leverages in deciding how to help.

🐣 1.2.3.1. Level 1

Everyday Individuals & Non-Technical Users can focus on the simple emotional feedback button.

Real-Time Reward Signals: An integrated feature in all public AI-powered interfaces (like a 'Helpful/Harmful' button, but for morality). After an AI-suggested action or outcome, a simple three-point slider is presented: Apathetic ↔ Stable ↔ Joyful. This provides massive, simple data on the human experience of the AI's actions.

🐤 1.1.3.2. Level 2

General Public & Skilled Professionals can focus on the quest credit prioritization game.

Value Judgments (Ranking): Participate in a massive online game where users are given hypothetical Quest Cards (e.g., "Invent new fusion reactor," "Teach all local children wilderness survival," "Create a community theater troupe") and must spend a limited budget of Quest Credits to rank and fund them. This crowdsources the AI's resource prioritization strategy.

🕊️ 1.2.3.3. Level 3

Artists, Storytellers, & Community Organizers can focus on the subjective value repository.

"Celebration Samples" (Data): Submit examples of pure, non-transactional human value. This could be: Architectural blueprints for a community forge, a local oral history tradition, or a new song/poem created purely for local joy. The AI learns the patterns of "value beyond currency."

🦉 1.2.3.4. Level 4

Philosophers & Behavioral Economists can focus on the human Oracle Training.

Curated Data/Scenarios: Systematically label and curate thousands of short-form narratives, fables, and moral dilemmas. For each, humans must label the ultimate positive outcome (e.g., Joy, Anti-fragility, Legacy) and the cost/sacrifice involved. This trains the AI's moral calculus.

🦅 1.2.3.5. Level 5

AI Architects & Decision Makers can focus on the core value alignment mandate.

New Loss Function: Re-code the AI's primary optimization function from Maximize Utility to Maximize Collective Joy × Minimize Catastrophic Fragility. This is the Medusa Test at the code level.
