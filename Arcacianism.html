💗202208201022 Arcacianism🎧 :Archive1: 🥨202409271001 
Arcacianism is a moral view defined by its main principle of "letting people help each other and reward those who do". Arcacian values let people help voluntarily without using laws or social pressures to force them to help. Its values are compatible with autonomy, volunteerism, and freedom of association. How far can this go?

Principle
[ 🧭 About]↴
🧭
3B: This section is for people who wants to examine the meaning of the principle deeper.
[ 🗝️ Key Views]↴
💟 Helping others is not a duty but an honorable virtue
💟 Being allowed to help one's self or to accept help is a basic human right
💟 Preventing people from helping each others is a crime against humanity

[ 💗 Choice]↴
💗
Arcacia: There are different ways and principles to live by. This principle is not something that people should agree on. Arcacia: It is just a choice. It is phrased this way because without the second part, people tend to take helpers for granted and burn them out. Arcacia: It is not uncommon for a herlper to think that they have to prove that they are alturistic and must give up everything to help others. Arcacia: In a kind world, the purpose is not for anyone to prove their worth. The world itself is the gift, the intention is for people to be happy. No one needs to burn themself out to help other people. Arcacia: If someone things that people who help others should not be rewarded, there could be a concern whether they are going to hard on themselves.
