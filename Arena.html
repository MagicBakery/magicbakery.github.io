Philosophy Arena

1. About

Welcome to the Philosophy Arena where different schools of thoughts battle it out for peace. Which will survive humanity and emerge victorious?

🌟 1.1. Philosophy x Entertainment

The purpose of Philosophy Arena is to make philosophical comparisons and debates visual, entertaining, and memorable, and by doing so attracts greater interest in philosophical refinement toward the best, undefeated philosophical framework.

Imagine that the debaters are champions of their philosophy. If the debate or comparison is a simulated fight, what whould happen?

3. Champions

These are predefined characters for recurring battles.

🍮 3.1. Little Red Riding Hood

Little Red Riding Hood the Baker is the Guild Master of Magic Bakery, which hosts the writing of Quest Society Ethics to import to the Earth.

She believes that the ultimate ethics cannot just be good; it must also be cute, because Cute Is Justice.

:Paladin: 3.1.1. Combat Skills

Her combat abilities are:

Unlimited Breadsticks raining down from the sky;

Cakescape: A skill where an attacker discovers that what was hit was just a cake sculture, as in the show "Is It Cake?";

Summoning animated pastries such as Giant Puddings;

Bagel Bazooka: Shoots an extremely hard primeval bagel using Hestia's Fire representing the dawn of human civilization when human tamed fire, using it both for security and celebration.

Although she can summon and control fire, Little Red Riding Hood does not use fire to fight because "Hestia's Fire is not for baking."

:Phoenix: 3.1.2. Red Cloak

Little Red Riding Hood's Red Cloak can absorbs or insulate fire from both outside and inside.

This magically allows Little Red Riding Hood to be a walking oven.

🧺 3.1.3. Picnic Basket

Little Red Riding Hood's Picnic Basket is not an "item box" but a a protal to Magic Bakery, letting her get things from Magic Bakery.

The expected use is to get pastries or cooking supplies.

But since Magic Bakery is a guild house, who knows what else guild members left there?

🐾 3.1.4. Sirius

Little Red Riding Hood can summon a big white Samoyed dog named Sirius to ride on.

She thinks that Sirius is a wolf. But others say it is a Samoyed because it is too cute and smiley.

Sirius is often seen going with others guild members, and is known to guide lost souls to Magic Bakery.
